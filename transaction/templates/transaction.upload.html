Transaction Sheet


This site accepts the CSV output from Mint.com. Once logged in to Mint, go to the "Transactions" tab. At the bottom of the list of transactions, there will be a link that says, "Export all n transactions". Click there to download a CSV that you can upload here.

Note: You should format the date column in the CSV before uploading it.

Open in Excel > highlight dates > right-click > format > custom > type in YYYY-MM-DD
